OpenTreatments envisions a society where every rare disease will have at least one treatment accessible to every patient around the world.

OpenTreatments Foundation is a non-profit organization started by patients, drug development experts, academic researchers, and industry partners to make this vision a reality. We are building the world's first Open Source Software Platform to supercharge patient foundations to create treatments for their own diseases.
